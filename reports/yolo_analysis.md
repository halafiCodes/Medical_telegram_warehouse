## Limitations of using a pre-trained model (YOLOv8 COCO)
- Trained on generic objects, not medical products.
- Products can be misclassified (e.g., bottles/cups) or missed.
- Lacks domain/context understanding.
- Sensitive to lighting/angle/quality.
- Heuristic categories (promotional/product_display/lifestyle) are approximate.
